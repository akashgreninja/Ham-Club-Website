Events for Manthan

1) KGF - The Game :

Event Details :-

❖ Event Duration : 2 hours

❖ Registration fee : Rs. 100/- per person

❖ Location : Level 1, Level 2 - Main Building, Final Round - Quadrangle

❖ Prize :-

1st Place: Gift Hampers Worth 5000/-

2nd Place: Gift Hampers Worth 3000/-

The event is about the movie KGF (both the chapters)

Eg: Chapter 1.1 and Chapter 2.1 would be a quiz on the movies

The further levels can have something like:

⮚ Pick a chit and say the dialogue; or enact a skit (from kgf)

⮚ Finding El Dorado (The final level of 1.3 would be a game to gold biscuit )

⮚ Final round of 2.3 would be a director level one, in which the participant should give a glimpse of chapter 3 based on his/her idea.

Based on the overall performance, the winners would be decided, and provided with gift hampers. Come win your "Duniya"

2) STUDENT OF THE YEAR :

Event Details :-

❖ Event Duration : 3 hours

❖ Registration fee : Rs. 150/- per person

❖ Location : Main Building (one classroom on each floor), Finals - Quadrangle

❖ Prize :-

Title Winner : Gift Hampers worth 7000/-

Runner up: Gift Hampers worth 4000/-

Each floor of the college building would be considered as a level in the game

Starting off with the 7th floor, people who can make it until the quadrangle would be the finalists for the title.

Example of one of the level would be :

A board game where the participant would be a pawn, and each number on the board game would

have a unique activity and those who complete the game first getting through the board game will make

it to

the next level.

The winner will be Entitled with "Student of the Year"

Along with gift hampers!

3) FILM CRAFT :

Event Details :-

❖ Event Duration : 1 hour

❖ Registration fee : Rs. 200/- per team

❖ Location : Preliminary Round - Corridor, Finals - Main stage

❖ Awards :-

Best Performer

Best Team

Each team should select a movie of their choice

Dress up like the cast from the selected movie

Each team will get around 5 mins of stage presence

In this given amount of time they can either perfom:

● A Scene

● A Song

● A Dance

● A couple of Dialogues

All of the above from the selected movie*

Judgement would be done based on the overall performance, and the winners will be awarded.
